Weekly Workshops

Learn web development on campus at our Thursday night workshops.

You'll learn at your own pace and we'll provide the resources to help you do it.

Portfolio Contest

We're held a campus-wide contest in the spring 2014 semester and gave away two great prize packs — one from a student with prior experience, one without — to the students that created the best portfolios.

Each prize pack included:

  • Three free domain names from Namecheap - Turn your ideas to life with coupons for three free domain names from Namecheap!
  • Six months of Spotify premium - Play any song on any device, download music and listen offline in high-quality audio without ads.
  • One year of TreeHouse - Learn everything from HTML to iPhone development with TreeHouse’s great videos.
  • HTML5 Foundations and CSS3 Foundations - Two great e-books from TreeHouse about HTML5 and CSS3.
  • $25 at Moes - Take a break from coding and treat yourself to a burrito!
  • Signed copy of Without Their Permission - An inspiring book signed by Alexis Ohanian, the book’s author and co-founder of Reddit.

The two best portfolios were created by Tyler Constance and Brian Barrett and chosen by three local web and media professionals.
